Pitched Roofing (New Roofs & Re-roofing)

A professionally installed pitched roof provides timeless appeal and robust protection. We are experts in both creating brand new roof structures and complete re-roofing projects, replacing old, worn-out coverings with modern, high-performance systems.

  • Slate & Tile Roofing: We work with natural slate, clay tiles, and concrete tiles to deliver a classic, durable finish with a lifespan of decades.
  • Structural Work: Our service includes timber work, joist replacement, and ensuring the underlying structure is sound before new coverings are applied.
  • Insulation & Ventilation: We ensure your new roof meets modern standards for thermal efficiency and airflow, reducing energy bills and preventing condensation.

Flat Roofing Solutions

Modern flat roofing is a versatile and cost-effective solution for extensions, garages, balconies, and commercial buildings. We install cutting-edge systems that are completely waterproof, durable, and low-maintenance.

  • EPDM Rubber Roofing: A highly durable synthetic rubber membrane with a lifespan of over 50 years. It's installed in a single sheet, eliminating seams and the risk of leaks.
  • GRP Fibreglass Roofing: A seamless, hard-wearing system that creates a tough, waterproof laminate. It can be finished in various colours and is suitable for foot traffic.
  • High-Performance Felt: A modern, multi-layer torch-on felt system that offers excellent waterproofing and a long lifespan, far superior to traditional felt roofing.

Roof Repairs & Maintenance

Addressing roof damage promptly is essential to protect your property's integrity. Our rapid-response repair team can handle any issue, from minor fixes to emergency storm damage, preventing small problems from becoming major expenses.

  • Leak Detection & Repair: Using our expertise and AI-assisted drone inspections, we quickly locate the source of leaks and provide a lasting fix.
  • Tile & Slate Replacement: We replace cracked, slipped, or missing tiles and slates, matching them to your existing roof to ensure a seamless appearance.
  • Chimney & Leadwork: We carry out repointing, flashing repairs, and complete leadwork replacement to secure these vulnerable areas of your roof.

Guttering, Fascias & Soffits

A well-maintained roofline is your property's first line of defence against water ingress. We install, repair, and replace durable uPVC roofline products that protect your home and enhance its appearance without the need for regular maintenance.

  • Guttering Systems: We ensure your gutters are correctly aligned, securely fixed, and free from blockages to effectively channel rainwater away from your property.
  • Fascias & Soffits: We replace rotting timber with high-quality uPVC fascias and soffits, which protect the rafter ends and provide essential ventilation to your roof space.
  • Cladding: Enhance and protect your home's exterior with our range of durable and attractive uPVC cladding options.
